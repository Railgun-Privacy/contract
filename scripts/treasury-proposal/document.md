The huge technological advances by the contributors to this project have proven their ability and willingness to deliver cutting edge tools.

### Market Maker Program

Many large traders and exchanges are interested in RAIL and listing the RAIL token. They will require a market maker to guarantee order books.

The DAO representatives will select a market maker with a good reputation and low fees ( such as Wintermute or GSR) and loan them 2.25m RAIL tokens from the Treasury, with the requirement that they return them after 18 months to the Treasury or buy them at a fair market price.

### Remainder

The 2+ millions of DAI, RAIL tokens and other tokens remaining will be used as Active Governor Reward, which will go to actively staked DAO members. and these tokens will not be moved as part of this proposal, and will be treated by the next or upcoming proposal for fee distribution.
