# Treasury Budget Proposal: Protocol Development

While total decentralization is something we all strive for, we must contend with a reality: groups and individuals make up the early success of projects. Decentralization comes in progressive steps. 

While the primary funding and operating mechanism behind RAILGUN over the last 18 months is coming to an end, this presents us with a great opportunity to embark on our decentralization journey.

This proposal would fund contributors of RAILGUN (described as the "Project Team”). The very first segment of team financing after approval will be an Initial Funding Period of 4.5 months, with a DAO review period 6 weeks before its end date. The Initial Funding Period will require one-half of the financing distribution required by a full 9-month period, for a total of 1.1M DAI, distributed proportionally with regard to each distribution area.

Upon completion of this Initial Funding Period, full payments will commence at a cadence of every nine months, contingent to DAO review each six months following funding. The DAO review will take place in the stakers-only chat. There will be a published spending report, and an on-chain proposal to cancel and edit the future funding. Approval of the Cancel/Edit will cancel the next funding round, making room for a new proposal. Otherwise, funding will continue for the next period.

The RAILGUN Project is entering a high-growth phase and needs skilled engineering and promotional talents and budget to achieve the goal of 1 billion USD in daily volume through the system.

In order to further build the RAILGUN anonymity set, distribution funds will be shielded into the RAILGUN system within 48 hours of approval and held there to add anonymity set for the benefit all of users.

## Distribution Areas

### Team and contractors
1.85M DAI

Developer and project team payments for ongoing research and development into upgrades and utility. Supports at least 10 product engineering contributors and 2 operations contributors for 9 months.

### Integrations from other protocols
150k DAI

This will fund development of wallets such as the Railway Wallet and other third-party integrations. 

### Audits
200k DAI

Budget for external audits of new and upgraded features. This budget will support one full audit by a reputable firm that is familiar with zero-knowledge proving technology.

### Development goals

The Project Team has committed to the following points for the next 18 months (two product development payment cycles). This list will continue to expand on a weekly basis: 

- Fee distribution contract
- Mobile app release
- "RAILYSWAP": private internal atomic swaps 
- RAILGUN contract NFT support
- Private NFT storage (ie for private staking/LP)
- NFT frontend and private auction support
- Relayer network reliability (continual)
- Public release of Relayer and Lepton code 
- Internationalization and translation support
- Research on Ledger support from existing libraries 
- Deeper ZK circuit studies to reduce gas costs of complex transactions 
- "RAILGUN Connect": to allow dApps to integrate RAILGUN (similar to Metamask, WalletConnect) 
- DeFi integrations including but not limited to:
    - Ren
    - Badger
    - Curve
    - Convex
- EVM deployments:
    - Polkadot
    - Arbitrum (L2)
    - METIS (L2)
- Reimplementation of EVM contracts and libraries in Rust for deployment on:
    - Solana
    - Near

### Updates

The core contributors will develop a methodology to report quarterly burn rate, development progress and ongoing funding priorities. 

This includes cataloging activities in an open and transparent manner for the community, while continuing to protect the individual financial privacy of contributors. Core contributors will continue weekly DAO calls and provide regular updates in the DAOscord.

### Renewal

A "review and cancel" proposal after six months will give the DAO an option to reject continual funding if the updates or progress are not in line with expectations.

### Treasury

The Treasury contract will be upgraded with code to handle future Active Governor Rewards (fee distributions) and recurring payments.
